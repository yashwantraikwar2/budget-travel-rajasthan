Best Time to Visit Rajasthan on a Budget

Travel between October and March for pleasant weather. Book early for savings. Monsoon months (July–September) offer discounts up to 50%. Avoid Diwali and New Year — prices triple then.

Budget-Friendly Cities in Rajasthan

Jaipur

The Pink City offers hostels from ₹500 per night. Visit free attractions like Jal Mahal, Panna Meena ka Kund, and colorful bazaars. For groups, booking a tempo traveller helps split costs for sightseeing.

Udaipur

Stay around Lake Pichola for budget stays with views. Walk the ghats and skip costly boat rides.

Jodhpur

Dorm beds from ₹250. Enjoy rooftop views of Mehrangarh Fort for free while dining.

Pushkar

Rooms from ₹400. Visit ghats and temples free of charge. Avoid Camel Fair rush unless pre-booked.

Jaisalmer

Stay inside the fort for ₹500–700. Budget desert camps with camel rides start at ₹800.

Affordable Transportation in Rajasthan

Between Cities

  • RSRTC buses: Jaipur–Udaipur ₹300, Jodhpur–Jaisalmer ₹250
  • Trains: Sleeper class half the price of buses
  • Shared taxis: Faster, pay per seat

Within Cities

  • City buses under ₹20
  • Auto-rickshaws ₹10–15/km
  • Bicycle rentals ₹50–100/day

Cheap Accommodation Options

  • Hostels: Zostel, Moustache (₹200–400)
  • Budget Hotels: ₹500–800, near bus stands
  • Homestays: ₹400–700, often with breakfast
  • Guest Houses: Govt. PWD/Forest (₹300–500)

Food on a Budget

  • Street food: Dal Baati ₹60, Mirchi Vada ₹15, Lassi ₹30
  • Thali meals: ₹100–150, unlimited
  • Cook at hostels to save 70%

Free & Low-Cost Attractions

Visit stepwells like Chand Baori, markets like Johari Bazaar, and lakes such as Fateh Sagar for almost free. Join free walking tours and tip guides.

Jaipur → Pushkar → Udaipur → Jodhpur route. Stay in hostels, use RSRTC buses, and enjoy authentic food. Group travellers can hire a tempo traveller in Jaipur for multi-city trips.

Money-Saving & Safety Tips

  • Book 2–3 months in advance
  • Refill bottles from RO points
  • Eat where locals eat
  • Bargain smartly (start at 40%)
  • Use tourist police helpline: 1363
